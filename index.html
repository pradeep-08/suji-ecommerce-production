About Us

*Suji Hair and Makeup*offers a range of services to enhance your natural beauty for any occasion. From basic to HD, matte, and dewy makeup, we cater to all needs including engagement, reception, and pre-wedding looks. Whether it’s party makeup, bridal makeup, or special events like baby showers and puberty ceremonies, our expert team ensures you look stunning and radiant. Services

Reception makeup

For a flawless reception makeup look, use long-lasting primer, smooth foundation, defined eyeliner, dramatic mascara, glowing highlighter, and sophisticated lipstick. These essentials ensure you look radiant and picture-perfect all evening.

Baby shower makeup

For a baby shower, achieve a radiant look with hydrating primer, light foundation, soft neutral eyeshadow, mascara, subtle blush, and natural lip color. These essentials ensure a charming, elegant appearance.

Matte makeup

For a sleek, matte finish, use a mattifying primer, high-quality matte foundation, and translucent powder. Apply matte eyeshadows and finish with matte lipstick for a polished, long-lasting look.

Party makeup

Shine at your event with these party makeup tips: start with primer and vibrant foundation, use bold eyeshadows, eyeliner, and mascara, add highlighter to your cheeks, and finish with striking lipstick.

Pre wedding shoot

For a flawless pre-wedding shoot look, start with a smoothing primer and full-coverage foundation. Use long-wearing concealer, set with translucent powder, and enhance with defined eyeliner, volumizing mascara, and long-lasting lipstick.

Bridesmaid makeup

For gorgeous bridesmaid makeup, start with primer and matching foundation. Use a versatile eyeshadow palette, define with eyeliner and mascara, add blush, and finish with long-lasting lipstick for a polished look.

Groom makeup

For a polished groom look, use a mattifying primer and lightweight foundation. Apply concealer, set with translucent powder, define brows, add neutral eyeshadow, and finish with a natural lip tint.
